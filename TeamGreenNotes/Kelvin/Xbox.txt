The original Xbox has a Kelvin GPU.
The xbox is easy to get a framebuffer setup for, as 
the framebuffer lies extremely high in the memory.
(the last couple MBs of memory); Mean we can use it quite easily
without dealing with the GPUs registers much.

Some seriously good information can be grabbed here:
https://xboxdevwiki.net/Porting_an_Operating_System_to_the_Xbox_HOWTO#Video_Driver
